Welcome to iStock

iStock provides users with an interactive visualization of the development of different securities over time. It offers a dynamic bar chart race feature, allowing users to select stocks and watch in an engaging way which stock performs best over a chosen timeframe. Additionally, iStock includes a simple comparison tool that presents a detailed breakdown of selected stocks, including Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume (OHLCV) data, giving users a clear and informative view of stock performance.

Bar Chart Race: The Bar Chart race feature shows the market cap of stocks during your selected time period across a scaled graph.

Bollinger Bands: This feauture is a line chart with bollinger bands with an adjustable period window and standard deviation to compare stock trends

Line Chart: This feature shows stock performance through a percent change line chart graph with a logarithmic y-axis. You can adjust the slider to compare performance over time.
